Plot2D demo

This demo illustrates the use of the Geotoolkit.org Plot2D widget by drawing random data. The Plot2D widget extends ZoomPane and inherits its zoom capabilities.

Widget javadoc: Plot2D
How to make your own: Create an instance of Plot2D. Define the axis labels with the add[X|Y]Axis(String) methods. Finally invoke the addSeries(label, color, x, y) method or one of its variants.
Prerequites:
  • Java 6

Note that the applet is known to not work on MacOS.
